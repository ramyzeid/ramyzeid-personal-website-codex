---
title: "Why the Care Economy Matters Now"
date: "2026-01-15"
summary: "A short note on why demographic transition makes care policy central to growth and inclusion."
tags:
  - "Care Economy"
  - "Policy"
---

Demographic transition is changing both **who needs care** and **who can provide it**. Policy systems that still assume unlimited unpaid care within households are becoming less realistic.

Three implications follow:

1. Childcare and eldercare should be modeled jointly.
2. Labor market policy should internalize care constraints.
3. Administrative data systems should track care-related vulnerabilities over time.
