title: "A Checklist for Policy Briefs that Actually Get Used"
date: "2025-06-20"
summary: "A compact checklist for writing briefs that survive decision timelines."
tags:
  - "Communication"
  - "Policy"
---

Policy briefs fail when they optimize for completeness over decision utility.

A brief is useful if it answers:

- What decision is pending?
- What is the minimum evidence needed today?
- What are the implementation constraints?
